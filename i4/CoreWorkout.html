WARMUP

Warm-up:

Plank - Hold for 30 seconds
Cat-Cow Stretch - 10 repetitions
Bird Dog - 10 repetitions per side
Standing Side Bends - 10 repetitions per side
Torso Twists - 10 repetitions per side

WORKOUT

Calisthenics Core Workout:

Bicycle Crunches - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
Mountain Climbers - 3 sets of 12-15 reps per leg
Russian Twists - 3 sets of 12-15 reps per side
Flutter Kicks - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
Plank Jacks - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Gym Core Workout:

Cable Crunches - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Medicine Ball Slams - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Hanging Leg Raises - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Weighted Russian Twists - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per side
Swiss Ball Rollouts - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

COOLDOWN

Cool-down:

Child's Pose - Hold for 30 seconds
Seated Forward Fold - Hold for 30 seconds
Standing Side Stretch - 10 repetitions per side
Supine Figure 4 Stretch - Hold for 30 seconds per side
Deep Breathing - 2 minutes
