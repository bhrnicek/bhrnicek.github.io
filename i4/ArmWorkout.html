WARMUP

Warm-up Routine:

Jumping Jacks - 2 minutes
Arm Circles - 30 seconds
Shoulder Rolls - 30 seconds
Wrist Circles - 30 seconds
Push-up to Downward Dog - 5 reps
Arm Swings - 30 seconds

WORKOUT

Callisthenic Arm Workout:

Push-ups - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Diamond Push-ups - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Tricep Dips - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Chin-ups - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Inverted Rows - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Handstand Push-ups (against a wall or assisted) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Plank - 3 sets of 30-60 seconds (for core stability)

Gym Arm Workout:

Barbell Bicep Curls - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Dumbbell Hammer Curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Tricep Dips (with added weight) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Skull Crushers (using an EZ bar or dumbbells) - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Cable Tricep Pushdowns - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Barbell Preacher Curls (using a preacher curl bench) - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Cable Bicep Curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

COOLDOWN

Cool-down Routine:

Shoulder and Tricep Stretches - 30 seconds each side
Wrist Flexor and Extensor Stretches - 30 seconds each side
Cat-Cow Stretch - 1 minute
Child's Pose - 1 minute
Deep Breathing - 2 minutes
