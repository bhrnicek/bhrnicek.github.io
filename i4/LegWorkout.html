WARMUP

Warm-up Routine:

Jogging in place - 2-3 minutes
Leg swings - 10 swings each leg (forward and backward)
Hip circles - 10 circles clockwise and 10 circles counterclockwise
Walking lunges - 10 lunges each leg
Squat jumps - 10 jumps

WORKOUT

Calisthenic Leg Workout:

Bodyweight Squats - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Lunges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
Bulgarian Split Squats - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
Jumping Squats - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Glute Bridges - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Calf Raises - 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Gym Leg Workout:

Barbell Squats - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Leg Press - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Dumbbell Lunges - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
Leg Extensions - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Hamstring Curls - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
Standing Calf Raises - 3 sets of 12-15 reps

COOLDOWN

Cool-down Routine:

Standing quad stretch - 30 seconds each leg
Standing hamstring stretch - 30 seconds each leg
Seated butterfly stretch - 30 seconds
Standing calf stretch - 30 seconds each leg
Full-body stretches (e.g., downward dog, child's pose) - 1 minute
