WARMUP

Warm-up:

Cat-Cow Stretch - 10 repetitions
Arm Circles - 10 repetitions forward and backward
Standing Side Bends - 10 repetitions per side
Torso Twists - 10 repetitions per side
Shoulder Rolls - 10 repetitions forward and backward

WORKOUT

Calisthenics Back Workout:

Push-ups - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Superman - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Reverse Snow Angels - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Bird Dog - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per side
Plank with Arm Row - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per side
Gym Back Workout:

Lat Pulldowns - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Bent-Over Rows - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Seated Cable Rows - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Deadlifts - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
T-Bar Rows - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

COOLDOWN

Cool-down:

Child's Pose - Hold for 30 seconds
Seated Forward Fold - Hold for 30 seconds
Standing Side Stretch - 10 repetitions per side
Supine Figure 4 Stretch - Hold for 30 seconds per side
Deep Breathing - 2 minutes
